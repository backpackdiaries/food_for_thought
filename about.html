Analisa is a Los Angeles-raised artist 

who studied fine art at The Cooper Union 

in New York City. Her practice spans 

a variety of mediums that ranges from 

fashion, graphic design, sculpture, 

fine art, and video and sound. 

Her mode of making usually revolves 

around projects {conceptual and commercial} 

exsisting in physical as well as digital 
 
spaces. She is currently living and 

working in Los Angeles, California.
